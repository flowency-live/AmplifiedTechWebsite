# How We Deliver Automation

## Step 1  - Identify Your High-Value Automation Opportunities  
We sit with you, map your workflows, and highlight where minutes and hours are being wasted.

Immediate red flags include:
- Double entry  
- Manual notifications  
- Offline spreadsheets  
- Job progress hidden across tools  
- Single-person knowledge bottlenecks  
- Tasks requiring copy-paste  

## Step 2  - Build Automations Fast  
Most automations are delivered in **1–3 days**, depending on complexity.

We use:
- AI  
- No-code  
- Custom scripts  
- System connectors  
- Micro-apps  
- Webhooks and event-driven automation  

Whatever removes the waste fastest.

## Step 3  - Monitor, Improve, Expand  
We revisit the automations after a few days or weeks to:
- Remove friction  
- Improve accuracy  
- Add extra steps or AI logic  
- Link into dashboards  

You can call in improvements through the credit system at any time.
